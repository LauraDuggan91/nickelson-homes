About Us

NICKELSON HOMES Ltd. specializes in building custom designed homes to match your individuality and style. We take great pride in our craftsmanship and our ability to work closely with our clients, providing solutions to suit all budgets.

Serving Toronto and vicinity, we have designed, built and renovated homes since 1990. Our wealth of experience and attention to detail, match the high expectations our clients want and deserve. By dealing directly with the builder you are ensured a smooth and satisfying building experience.

We provide highly skilled project management including a comprehensive budgeting process. We have thorough knowledge of and stay up-to- date with the industry and Ontario Building Code. Through well-established relationships with architects, designers, quality trade partners and suppliers we provide a tradition of trust, collaboration and customer satisfaction.

Mission Statement

NICKELSON HOMES Ltd. is committed to working with and listening carefully to our clients. We ensure your full satisfaction by building quality custom homes that honour your vision and recognize the value of your investment.

02

Environmental Statement

NICKELSON HOMES Ltd. is an environmentally conscious company. With rising energy prices and wide spread environmental awareness, residential building is required to be more eco-friendly than ever. We believe that high-quality sustainable design can be integrated into our clients’ needs, desires and budget. We also support and participate in all areas of recycling where possible and donate all reusable materials from our renovation projects to Habitat for Humanity.
